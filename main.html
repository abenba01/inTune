What is inTune?

Everyday we ask ourselves, what do we want to listen to? The real question is why do you listen to it? You spend countless minutes searching for a playlist to fit your mood, but this becomes difficult when you can't pinpoint how you're feeling.
inTune takes care of this problem for you. We take external variables—such as weather, location, time of year and time of day—to determine your mood. We then use this affective information and your personal music preferences to make you a customized mix.
