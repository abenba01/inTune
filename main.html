What is inTune?

Why do you listen to what you listen to? Because of how you're feeling. You spend countless minutes searching for a playlist to fit your mood, but this becomes difficult when you can't pinpoint how you're feeling.
inTune takes care of that problem for you. We take external variables—such as weather, location and time of day—to determine your mood. We then use this information to make you a customized mix based on your personal music preferences
Happy listening,
the inTune team
